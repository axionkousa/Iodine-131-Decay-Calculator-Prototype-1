# Iodine-131-Half-Life-Simulation-Prototype-1

This is a simulation (or rather a _calculator_) to determine the final mass of the substance iodine-131 after some period of time.

## Keynote to know.
- Iodine-131 has a half-life of 8 days.

## How does it work?
It takes inputs of the initial mass in _grams_ and the duration of elapsed time in _days_, then calculates them with the formula '$N(t) = N_0 \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{\frac{t}{t_{1/2}}}$'
